Voice cues announce every phase and count you in for the last 5 seconds, so you don't need to watch the screen — keep your music playing in the background. While a session is running you'll see a quiet "Jog / Walk Timer" media notification; that's what keeps the voice working with your screen locked, without ever forcing your screen to stay on. Your music will dip slightly during a session and ease back in when you stop (Android does this automatically to make room for the voice cues). Note: 100% here is the maximum volume the browser allows for spoken cues — if it's still hard to hear over your music, raising your phone's overall media volume will have more effect than anything the app can do.
